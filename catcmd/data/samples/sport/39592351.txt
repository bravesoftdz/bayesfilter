Rangers: Dave King faces legal action after defying takeover ruling on share
King led a successful boardroom takeover at Ibrox in 2015
Rangers chairman Dave King faces a legal fight after defying an order to make
an offer for all shares in the club's holding company.
The Takeover Appeal Board (Tab) ruled last month that King had "worked in
concert" with three others to take control at Ibrox in 2015.
King did not meet Wednesday's deadline to make an offer of 20p per share.

King could also face "financial cold shouldering", which means no city
investors or institutions are allowed to deal with him.
