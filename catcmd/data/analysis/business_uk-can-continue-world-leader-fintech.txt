Fintech is changing the way we live and, in doing so, having a profound effect
on economies globally. Today this revolution means that you can pay your bus
fare by touching a card to a machine, you can book a taxi on your phone with
the swipe of a finger, and you can transfer money across the world using a
screen in your kitchen. This would have been unthinkable even a decade ago.
